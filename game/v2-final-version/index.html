How to play

Goal: be the first player to reach 30 points.

  • On your turn, click Draw to reveal two tiles (1–6).
  • If you draw two 1s: your score resets to 0 and the turn passes.
  • If you draw one 1: you gain 0 points and the turn passes.
  • Otherwise, the tile values are added to your score. You can draw again or pass.

Shortcuts: M = mute, I = info, D = draw, P = pass, R = restart round
