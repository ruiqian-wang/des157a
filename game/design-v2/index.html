Game On Design V2

I made the “Play Again” and “Pass” buttons more obvious, so users can clearly see that they are clickable buttons rather than just descriptions or design elements. I also added labels for Player 1 and Player 2 to distinguish between the players.

When a player wins, the game now displays the Chinese term for winning in Mahjong (“胡了”) along with the English text “Player is winning.” A marker points to the winning player, and a “Play Again” button has also been added.
